Our Values

Believe.

We believe in the transformative power of faith and the limitless possibilities that come through Christ. Guided by Matthew 19:26, "With God all things are possible," we inspire hope and confidence in His plan.

Connect.

We are dedicated to fostering meaningful connections—between individuals, families, and with God. Through fellowship, worship, and shared experiences, we create a space where everyone feels welcomed and valued.

Grow.

BGP is a place of spiritual and personal growth where transformation is nurtured. Together, we grow in faith, love, and purpose, becoming a light that uplifts our community and beyond.
